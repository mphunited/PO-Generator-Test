📋 How to Import Orders:   1) For single orders, click the order and then click Load into PO Builder.   2) For split orders, click all the orders and then click Load as Split Load.   3) For split loads that need multiple PO numbers on one PO, click all the orders (up to 4) and then click Split Load/Multiple PO Numbers.   4) For multiple independent POs, click all the orders (up to 20) and then click the Generate Multiple POs button.   5) If you're doing Recycling Orders, click Recycling Orders.   5) Use the filter to see orders from a given vendor, or use the search to find your order. (If your order is not showing up, make sure the status is PO Request to Accounting, PO Revision to Accounting, or PO Moving on the Excel sheet and/or click Refresh — sometimes it takes a few minutes to pull data.)

📂 How to Import PO Requests:
1. Click Import PO Requests from Excel.
2. PO generates automatically. But if you make any changes to any of the fields, click Generate PO at the bottom.
3. If you are Revising a PO or have a Blind Shipment, check the appropriate box — the PO will regenerate automatically.
📋 How to send the Vendor Email with PO attached:
1. Click Save PO PDF and save the PO to a folder on your computer.
2. Click Vendor Email, then click Copy HTML Body to copy the email content.
3. Click Open Email, paste into the body of the email, then attach the saved PDF.
